# Operation PAWS: National Breeder League — Code of Conduct & Promotion Rules

## 1. The "highly secure" Integrity Charter
The Breeder League is a meritocracy. Bragging rights and Tier 1 status cannot be bought or influenced. They are earned through the transparent aptitude performance of the K9 assets.

## 2. Breeder Tiers & Definitions
- **Tier 3 (T3) - Academy/Youth**: Grassroots breeders, youth clubs, and learning handle initiatives. Focus is on fundamental socialization and basic drive.
- **Tier 2 (T2) - Professional/Commercial**: Established breeders supplying private security or sport. Focus is on stability and high-level obedience.
- **Tier 1 (T1) - National Tactical**: The elite. Only breeders who supply the SAPS for narcotics, explosives, and tracking work. Focus is on extreme drive and high-stakes reliability.

## 3. Promotion & Relegation Rules (Automated)
The **AI Council** monitors these rules 24/7. 

### From Tier 3 to Tier 2:
- **Requirement**: Successfully register 10 dogs with an average aptitude score of >70%.
- **Condition**: Zero Welfare Integrity violations for 12 consecutive months.
- **Action**: Automatic promotion in the National Tracker.

### From Tier 2 to Tier 1:
- **Requirement**: Successfully register 5 dogs with an "Elite Tactical" aptitude (>95%).
- **Condition**: Pass a physical facility audit by the SPCA and a financial audit by PwC.
- **Action**: Elevated to "National Tactical Supplier" status.

### Relegation (Loss of Bragging Rights):
- **Trigger**: Any single "Critical" welfare violation results in immediate relegation to Tier 3 and a 24-month ban on T1 supply.
- **Trigger**: Falling below an 85% average aptitude for two consecutive seasons results in Tier 2 status.

## 4. The "Round Robin" Audit
Every promotion is reviewed by the **Council of AIs**:
1. **The Auditor** verifies the data integrity.
2. **The Welfare Officer** verifies the SPCA compliance.
3. **The Strategist** verifies the tactical need.
4. **The Chair** signs the promotion certificate.
