# Operation PAWS: Master Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)
## Version 2.0 — Renegotiated Tactical Framework

### 1. Parties
This MOU is entered into by:
- **The National K9 Task Force** (Lead Auditor)
- **The South African Police Service (SAPS)** (Strategic Deployment)
- **The Participating Corporate Sponsors** (Financial Escrow)
- **The Council of Paws (COP)** (Autonomous AI Governance)

### 2. Autonomous Governance (The AI Council)
By signing this MOU, all parties agree that the day-to-day management, auditing, and promotion within the National Breeder League is governed by the **Council of Paws (COP)**. 
- No human official has the power to unilaterally override the AI Council's findings regarding welfare violations or aptitude failures.
- Decision-making follows a **Round Robin Consensus** between the Auditor AI, Welfare AI, and Strategist AI.

### 3. Financial Ring-Fencing
All sponsorship funds are held in a **Smart Escrow** system. Funds are only released for training or deployment once the AI Council has verified the candidate's PAWS-ID and SPCA compliance.

### 4. Public Transparency Ledger
Every dog's career, from T3 intake to T1 deployment, is recorded on an immutable ledger. This data is public and auditable via the [National Transparency Tracker](https://nicodemussibiya-dot.github.io/operation-paws/tracker/).

### 5. Termination of MOU
Failure to adhere to the AI Council's welfare standards results in the immediate withdrawal of corporate funding and the cessation of tactical asset deployment.
