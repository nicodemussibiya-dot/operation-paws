# 👮 POLICE BRIEFING QUICK-REFERENCE
## Operation PAWS: Institutional Continuity & Transparency

**FOR USE DURING OFFICIAL INQUIRIES / AUDITS**

### 1. SYSTEM INTEGRITY
- **Audit Logs**: Every system action is recorded in the `paws_audit_log`. These logs are immutable.
- **Role Control**: Access is strictly limited by role (Commissioner, Auditor, etc.) via Row Level Security (RLS).
- **MFA Required**: All high-level authorizations require 2FA (TOTP) verification.

### 2. DATA PROVENANCE
- **Microchip Verification**: Every dog is verified against the National Animal Identification System.
- **Vetted Inputs**: Welfare data must come from certified veterinarians with recorded license numbers.
- **Escrow Control**: Funds are only released upon verifiable milestone completion.

### 3. AI EXPLAINABILITY
- **Evidence Pyramid**: All AI decisions include a Conclusion, Data Source, Methodology, and Confidence score.
- **No Black Boxes**: The logic used by the AI is based on pre-defined policy documents and real-time database queries.
- **Human Oversight**: Significant decisions require a human "Double-Check" with 2FA.

### 4. COMPLIANCE
- **POPIA**: PII is encrypted at rest and only accessible via 2FA-verified audit roles.
- **SAPS Standards**: Training and welfare protocols align with national K9 standards.
