# Media Department: Visual Content Prompts (The "Deterrence" Series)

To "wow" partners and deter criminals, the Media Department should produce content following these high-impact prompts. Use these for AI Video Generation or as a script for a film crew.

## Prompt 1: The "Invisible" Hunter (Deterrence)
*   **Visual**: A pitch-black forest at night. Grainy green Night-Vision (NODs) footage.
*   **Action**: A suspect is running through the brush. Suddenly, a bright heat-signature (Thermal) appears from above—the CSIR Overwatch Drone. The drone locks on. Two seconds later, a K9 in a glowing tactical vest (LED ID) enters the frame at full speed.
*   **Sound**: Deep rhythmic bass, the low hum of a drone, and the sound of dry leaves crunching.
*   **Message**: "You can't hide from the sync."

## Prompt 2: The "Bavarian" Arrival (National Pride)
*   **Visual**: Slow-motion 4K footage of a cargo plane landing at sunset. The ramp lowers. 
*   **Action**: An International Donor dog (Rex) walks down the ramp. He is greeted by a SAPS handler. They exchange a "BRICS Universal Hand Signal."
*   **Sound**: Orchestral swells, the "Song of PAWS" jingle fading in.
*   **Message**: "Global Genetics. National Security."

## Prompt 3: The "Academy League" Finals (Transparency)
*   **Visual**: A high-tech training facility. Multiple dogs in different branded vests (Standard Bank, Hollard).
*   **Action**: A montage of dogs clearing obstacles, finding narcotics in hidden compartments, and ignoring "distraction meat."
*   **Sound**: High-energy industrial beats.
*   **Message**: "Only the Top 1% make the frontline."
