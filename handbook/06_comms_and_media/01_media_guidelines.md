# Media & Communications Guidelines

Operation PAWS is a high-visibility project. Every post and press release must reflect the project's values and protect its security.

## 1. Key Messaging
- **"Welfare-First"**: Always lead with the dog's well-being.
- **"Traceable & Anonymized"**: We show the process, but we protect the people.
- **"Not an Official SAPS Channel"**: Use the standard disclaimer on all public materials.

## 2. Filming Rules
- **Faces & Badges:** Blur all SAPS staff faces and name tags unless explicit permission is granted.
- **Sensitive Backgrounds:** Ensure no tactical equipment, radio call-signs, or secure facility layouts are visible in the background.
- **Microchips:** Never show a full microchip number in a public video.

## 3. Crisis Comms
In the event of an allegation (e.g., smuggling):
- **Immediate Response:** "Operation PAWS follows a 100% microchip-traceable pipeline. All records are available for official audit."
- **Data Proof:** Reference the Public Tracker and the Anti-Smuggling Protocol.
