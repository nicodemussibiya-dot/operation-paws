# The PAWS Pipeline Overview

Operation PAWS uses a tiered approach to move dogs from public donors/breeders to official working service.

## The Three Tiers

### Tier 1: Digital Lead (Screening)
- **Status:** Potential candidate identified via WhatsApp/Telegram/Web.
- **Action:** Basic questionnaire, photos, and video of toy drive.
- **Goal:** Filter out dogs that are clearly unsuitable (age, breed, obvious fear).

### Tier 2: The "Combine" (Try-out)
- **Status:** Candidate invited to a physical screening day.
- **Action:** Health check (SPCA/Vet) + Temperament test (PAWS Coaches).
- **Outcome:** 
    - **A) Immediate Handover:** Dog is ready for SAPS assessment.
    - **B) Academy Candidate:** Dog has high potential but needs conditioning (Tier 3).
    - **C) Humane Exit:** Dog is not suitable; returned to owner or SPCA rehoming.

### Tier 3: PAWS Academy (Conditioning)
- **Status:** High-potential dog undergoing foundation training.
- **Action:** 4-12 weeks of environmental conditioning and drive development.
- **Goal:** Prepare the dog to pass the official SAPS assessment with 100% confidence.

## Chain of Custody
Every dog movement between tiers is logged using the [Anti-Smuggling Protocol](../09_traceability/01_anti_smuggling_protocol.md) and tracked via the [Public Reference Spec](../07_metrics_and_ids/01_paws_reference_spec.md).
