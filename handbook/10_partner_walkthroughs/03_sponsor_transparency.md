# Sponsor & Partner Journey: Transparency

This guide explains how corporate sponsors and partners like Gift of the Givers (GotG) interact with the project to ensure financial and animal integrity.

## 1. Funding a Lead (PAWS Ref)
- **Step 1:** Sponsor pledges funds for the "Carletonville 10" pilot.
- **Step 2:** Funds are held by GotG in a ring-fenced account.
- **Step 3:** Each funded dog is assigned a PAWS Reference (e.g., `PAWS 000001`).

## 2. Tracking Progress
- **Real-Time Data:** Sponsors can visit the [Public Tracker](../../docs/tracker/) to see the status of "their" funded dogs (e.g., "In Training" or "SAPS Accepted").
- **Verification:** Any stakeholder can use the [Verification Hub](../../docs/verify/) to confirm a dog's legitimacy using its public ID.

## 3. Auditable Reporting
- **End-of-Pilot Report:** Sponsors receive a summary report that links every Rand spent to a specific, microchip-verified dog and its outcome.
- **Welfare Assurance:** Sponsors have proof that every dog was screened by an independent SPCA inspector, ensuring their brand is never linked to animal welfare controversies.
