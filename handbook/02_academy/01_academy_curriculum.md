# PAWS Academy Curriculum (Tier 3)

The PAWS Academy provides a **foundation-only, non-tactical** training environment. We do NOT teach aggression or specific police tactics. We focus on building the "raw material" that SAPS evaluators look for.

## 1. Environmental Conditioning
Exposure to various stimuli to ensure a stable, confident dog:
- **Surfaces:** Metal grates, slippery tiles, rubble, vibrating platforms.
- **Heights:** Open-backed stairs, cat-walks, jumping onto platforms.
- **Audio:** Sirens, crowds, loud noises (non-gunfire), vehicles.
- **Darkness:** Confident movement in pitch-black rooms and confined spaces.

## 2. Drive Development
Harnessing natural instincts for professional use:
- **Search Foundation:** Building an obsession with a specific toy (ball/tug).
- **Hunt Drive:** Searching for hidden toys in increasingly complex environments.
- **Stamina:** Building the focus to search for extended periods (15-20 mins).

## 3. Socialization & Neutrality
- **Human Neutrality:** The dog must be approachable and calm around strangers in various uniforms.
- **Dog Neutrality:** The dog must ignore other dogs while working.
- **Crowd Handling:** Moving through busy public spaces without distraction.

## 4. Basic Control (Obedience)
- **Recalls:** Immediate return to the handler.
- **Positions:** Sit, Down, Stand (on command, even at a distance).
- **Leash Manners:** Walking calmly on a loose lead.
- **Crate Training:** Relaxing in a travel crate/kennel between sessions.

## 5. Welfare & Grooming
- **Handling:** Tolerating vet checks, ear cleaning, and paw handling.
- **Conditioning:** Basic physical fitness and muscle building.
