# PAWS Pilot Schedule (Carletonville 10)

This is the tentative schedule for the **Operation PAWS Pilot**. Dates are subject to confirmation by SAPS K9 National/Provincial leadership and partner availability.

## Phase 1: Lead Generation & Digital Intake
- **Dates:** 01 May 2026 – 15 May 2026
- **Activity:** Digital QR campaign (WhatsApp/Telegram) goes live. Initial screening of photos/videos.

## Phase 2: Public Try-out Days (The "Combine")
- **Event 1: Carletonville Pilot Screening**
    - **Date:** Saturday, 23 May 2026
    - **Location:** [Local SPCA or Partner Kennel Name]
    - **Activity:** Open day for pre-registered leads. Basic health check (SPCA) + Temperament test (PAWS Coaches).
- **Event 2: Second Call (Overflow/Review)**
    - **Date:** Saturday, 30 May 2026
    - **Location:** [TBD]

## Phase 3: SAPS Official Assessment
- **Date:** 08 June 2026 – 12 June 2026
- **Location:** SAPS K9 Academy (Roodeplaat) or Provincial Unit.
- **Activity:** Top 10 candidates presented to SAPS evaluators for official donation assessment (SAPS 505a).

## Phase 4: Academy Intake (Tier 3)
- **Date:** 15 June 2026
- **Activity:** For dogs that show high potential but need 4-8 weeks of environmental "polishing" before final SAPS handover.

## How to Attend
1. Scan the QR code or use the [Web Chat](../chat/).
2. Complete the initial questionnaire.
3. If your dog meets the [Selection Criteria](./02_selection_criteria.md), you will receive an invitation for a specific time-slot.
4. **No walk-ins permitted** (to ensure animal safety and crowd control).
