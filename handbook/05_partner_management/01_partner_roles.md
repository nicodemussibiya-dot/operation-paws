# Partner Roles & Communication

Operation PAWS succeeds only through collaboration. This document defines the clear boundaries between partners to avoid "mission creep" or confusion.

## 1. Coordinator (Foundation)
- **Role:** The central "switchboard" and administrator.
- **Authority:** Manages the pipeline, leads, and digital transparency.

## 2. SAPS K9 Unit
- **Role:** The end-user and technical authority.
- **Authority:** Sole authority on final dog acceptance and deployment.

## 3. SPCA / Animal Welfare
- **Role:** The welfare guardian.
- **Authority:** Can "veto" a dog's participation on welfare grounds at any stage.

## 4. Gift of the Givers
- **Role:** The credibility and funding partner.
- **Authority:** Manages the transparent allocation of ring-fenced funds.

## 5. Breeders & Donors
- **Role:** The source of excellence.
- **Authority:** Retain ownership of the dog until the final SAPS 505a is signed.
