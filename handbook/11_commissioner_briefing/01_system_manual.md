# Strategic Command Manual: Operation PAWS
**Confidential: For the National Commissioner & Authorized Personnel Only**

## 1. THE ARCHITECTURE OF POWER
Operation PAWS is built on a "Tiered Access" architecture. Not all personnel see all data. This ensures operational security and PII protection.

| Role | Access Level | Primary Interface | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Public** | Level 1 | [Start Hub] / [AI Chat] | Awareness & Initial Leads |
| **Field Officer** | Level 2 | [Field Intake App] | Digital Registration & On-site Verification |
| **Welfare (SPCA)** | Level 3 | [Welfare Scorecard] | Independent Veto Power (Animal Rights) |
| **Academy Lead** | Level 4 | [Academy Portal] | Training, Scoring, & League Management |
| **Commissioner** | **Level 10** | **[Command Center]** | **Final Tactical Approval / SAPS Placement** |

## 2. THE COMMAND WORKFLOW
How a dog moves from the street to the SAPS Frontline:

1.  **Intake**: Field Officer registers the dog via the "Dumb-Proof" App.
2.  **Welfare Screen**: Independent SPCA official clicks "Welfare Pass" (The "Veto Gate").
3.  **The Academy**: Dog enters a "Sponsorship League" (Narcotics, Explosives, etc.).
4.  **Tech Sync**: CSIR-Defense links the dog with a Ballistic Vest and Overwatch Drone.
5.  **Final Dossier**: A professional candidate profile appears in the **Commissioner's Command Center**.
6.  **The Decision**: The Commissioner views the photo/stats and clicks **APPROVE**.

## 3. ACCESS CONTROL (Security)
*   **Public Tracker**: Shows NO personal data. Only "Reference Numbers" (e.g., PAWS-26-001) for transparency.
*   **Field App**: Protected by a rolling **4-Digit PIN** (Managed by you).
*   **Command Center**: Restricted to the National Commissioner's secure terminal.

## 4. STRATEGIC LEVERAGE
*   **Insurance (MOU-12)**: Costs are reduced automatically when the Commissioner deploys "Tech-Synced" dogs.
*   **BRICS Interoperability**: Any dog with the **BUCS Badge** is ready for deployment across BRICS member states.
