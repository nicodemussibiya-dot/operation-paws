# Intake Day Runbook (The "Combine")

This document outlines the standard operational procedure for a physical screening day (Tier 2).

## 1. Setup (07:00 - 08:30)
- **Station 1: Reception:** Verification of PAWS Reference and owner identity.
- **Station 2: Vet/Welfare Station:** Table for physical check, microchip scanner, and scale.
- **Station 3: Testing Area:** Open space for toy drive and environmental tests.
- **Station 4: Results/Exit:** Private area for handing back dogs or signing Academy intake.

## 2. Arrival Protocol
1. Owner arrives and scans the "Arrival QR".
2. **Microchip Verification:** The first action is to scan the dog to ensure it matches the digital lead.
3. **Safety:** All dogs must remain on lead and in vehicles until called.

## 3. The 10-Minute Assessment
- **Physical (2 mins):** Weight, coat, teeth, and general health.
- **Social (2 mins):** Reaction to a neutral stranger.
- **Drive (6 mins):** Response to ball/tug in an open field and around a distraction.

## 4. Documentation
- Results are logged directly into the mobile-friendly PAWS Intake Form.
- Owner signs a "Participation Waiver" before testing begins.
