# PAWS Welfare & Ethics Charter

Operation PAWS is built on a **Welfare-First** foundation. Every dog in the pipeline is a sentient being and a partner, not a tool.

## 1. No-Harm Principle
- Training methods must be reward-based and humane.
- Negative reinforcement or aversive tools (prong collars, e-collars) are strictly prohibited in PAWS Academy environments.
- Dogs must have adequate rest, hydration, and nutrition at all times.

## 2. The "Humane Exit" Policy
If a dog is deemed unsuitable for working service at any stage:
- The dog is returned to its owner with a "Thank You" and welfare advice.
- If surrendered, the dog is placed in the official SPCA rehoming pipeline.
- No dog is "dumped" or left without a clear, safe outcome.

## 3. Anti-Coercion
- Donors and breeders must participate voluntarily.
- No pressure is placed on individuals to donate dogs they wish to keep.
- Clear disclosure of the "Irrevocable Donation" (SAPS 505a) step is mandatory.

## 4. Conflict of Interest
- No staff member or partner may profit personally from the sale or diversion of dogs within the pipeline.
- Transparency in all financial and animal movements is the primary defense against corruption.
