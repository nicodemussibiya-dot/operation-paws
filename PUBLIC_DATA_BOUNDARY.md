# Public Data Boundary — STRICT RULES

This repository is **PUBLIC** and indexed by search engines. Assume anything committed here is cached forever.

**NEVER COMMIT TO THIS REPOSITORY:**
* Phone numbers, email addresses, or physical addresses of donors, breeders, or volunteers.
* WhatsApp screenshots or chat logs.
* Microchip numbers tied to real identities.
* Signed documents, MOUs, or consent forms.
* SAPS-provided internal templates (including SAPS 505(a) donation deeds) unless SAPS explicitly clears publication in writing.
* Sensitive SAPS operational info, facility layouts, or tactical training methods.

**ALLOWED IN THIS REPOSITORY:**
* Blank templates with placeholders.
* Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and operational runbooks.
* Aggregate metrics definitions and public dashboard code.
* Policy alignment references (e.g., noting that SAPS 505(a) is the required form).
* Public-safe code, database schemas, and AI prompts.
