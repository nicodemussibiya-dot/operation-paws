# Operation PAWS — Disclaimer (Public)

**Last updated:** 23 April 2026

## 1) Not an official SAPS channel
Operation PAWS (PAWS — *Partnership for Animal Work & Service*) is an **independent public-interest initiative / proposal** and a public playbook repository.

- This repository, its website pages (GitHub Pages), QR codes, and chat links are **not** official SAPS communications.
- **No SAPS endorsement is implied** unless confirmed in writing by SAPS through the appropriate channels.

## 2) Operational authority + final decisions
Where SAPS is involved:
- **SAPS remains the process owner** for any official assessment, acceptance, custody, and training pathway.
- **SAPS alone decides** whether any dog is suitable or accepted into its pipeline.
- Operation PAWS cannot guarantee assessment outcomes, acceptance, training success, or deployment.

## 3) Welfare-first
Operation PAWS is welfare-first:
- Not every dog is suitable for working roles, and that is normal.
- Screening exists to protect the dog, the donor/breeder, SPCA capacity, and SAPS resources.

## 4) Public data boundary (critical)
This is a **public repository** and should be treated as permanently indexed/cached.

We do **not** publish:
- donor/owner personal information (names, phone numbers, addresses),
- microchip numbers linked to identities,
- signed documents,
- sensitive operational details.

See: `DATA_BOUNDARY.md`.

## 5) Donations and anti-scam policy
To protect the public:
- We will **never** ask you to send money to random “personal wallet addresses” or to individuals via DMs.
- Donation methods (when enabled) must be published only via the **official Operation PAWS hub page** and documented in `DONATIONS.md`.

If you believe you’ve encountered a scam or impersonation, do not send money. Use official links only.

## 6) No legal advice
Nothing in this repository is legal advice. All partners should obtain their own legal/operational guidance before signing agreements or conducting activities.

## 7) Trademarks / official marks (project rule)
Operation PAWS will not use official marks, badges, or insignia associated with SAPS (or any partner) unless explicit written permission is obtained from the relevant rights holder.

## 8) Contact
Public contact details should live on the QR hub site and/or an official email address when available.  
(Do not post phone numbers in issues, commits, or public pages.)
