# Checklist: Screening Day Operations (The "Combine")

This checklist ensures that every Tier 2 screening event is safe, traceable, and professional.

## 1. Pre-Event (48 Hours Before)
- [ ] **Venue Confirmation:** Ensure water, shade, and secure fencing are available.
- [ ] **Partner Sync:** Confirm attendance of SPCA inspectors and PAWS coaches.
- [ ] **Equipment Check:** 
    - [ ] 2x Microchip scanners (fully charged).
    - [ ] 2x Digital scales.
    - [ ] Toy bag (Brand new balls/tugs to avoid scent contamination).
    - [ ] First-aid kits (Human & K9).
- [ ] **Lead Review:** Final list of invited PAWS references downloaded for offline backup.

## 2. On-Site Setup (Morning of Event)
- [ ] **Zoning:** Clearly mark the "Reception", "Vet Check", and "Test Field" zones.
- [ ] **Bio-security:** Foot baths or spray for personnel moving between test areas.
- [ ] **Signage:** Deploy "Operation PAWS — Official Intake" posters with QR codes.

## 3. During the Event (Per Dog)
- [ ] **Arrival:** Verify PAWS Ref + Microchip Scan immediately.
- [ ] **Vet Check:** SPCA Inspector confirms health and welfare.
- [ ] **Testing:** 
    - [ ] Environmental confidence test.
    - [ ] Toy/Hunt drive test.
- [ ] **Outcome Logged:** Immediate digital entry in the tracker.
- [ ] **Owner Briefing:** Clear explanation of "Next Steps" or "Return of Custody".

## 4. Post-Event (Wrap Up)
- [ ] **Decontamination:** Clean all crates and equipment.
- [ ] **Data Sync:** Ensure all offline logs are synced to Supabase.
- [ ] **Partner Debrief:** 15-minute review of any incidents or "Red Flag" dogs.
- [ ] **SAPS Notification:** Send summary of top candidates to SAPS Liaison.
