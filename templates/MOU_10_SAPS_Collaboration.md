# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (PROPOSAL)
## Operation PAWS – SAPS K9 Collaboration Framework

**Parties:**
- **[Foundation / Coordinator legal name]** (“Coordinator”)
- **South African Police Service (SAPS) K9 Unit** (“SAPS K9”)

**Effective date:** ____ / ____ / 2026
**Term:** Ongoing / Pilot Phase

### 1. Purpose
To establish a collaborative framework where Operation PAWS mobilizes, screens, and prepares suitable working dog candidates for formal assessment and potential donation to the SAPS K9 Unit.

### 2. Operational Authority
Both parties acknowledge that:
- **SAPS remains the sole authority** for final assessment, acceptance, and deployment decisions.
- **SAPS processes (SAPS 505(a), SAPS 84)** are the final legal steps for any dog transfer.
- Operation PAWS provides a "pre-qualified pipeline" to reduce SAPS administrative and screening burden.

### 3. Traceability & Anti-Smuggling (Robust Clause)
Operation PAWS commits to a 100% traceable pipeline via the **Anti-Smuggling & Diversion Protocol**.
- Every dog presented to SAPS will have a verified microchip and a recorded history.
- SAPS will have access to the PAWS audit trail for any dog in the pipeline.

### 4. Limitation of Scope
This collaboration is strictly limited to the **Operation PAWS** initiative and the specific goal of mobilizing K9 assets. It does not imply a general partnership or endorsement of the Coordinator by SAPS outside of this initiative.

### 5. Media & Communications
Both parties will coordinate on public communications. No sensitive SAPS operational details, layouts, or tactics will be shared by Operation PAWS.

### 6. Welfare & Ethics
Dogs not accepted by SAPS will be managed according to the PAWS "Humane Outcomes" policy (Returned to owner or SPCA rehoming).
