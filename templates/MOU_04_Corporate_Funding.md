# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU)
## Operation PAWS – Corporate Funding Sponsor

**Parties:**
- **[Corporate Name]** (“Sponsor”)
- **Gift of the Givers Foundation** (“GotG”)
- **[Foundation / Coordinator legal name]** (“Coordinator”)

**Effective date:** ____ / ____ / 2026
**Term:** 12 months (or until pledge is fully allocated)

### 1. Purpose
To provide transparent, ring-fenced funding to support the sourcing and processing of suitable dogs for SAPS K9 capacity under Operation PAWS.

### 2. Funding flow (Auditable & Secure)
- Sponsor contributes funds directly to GotG (EFT only).
- GotG issues Section 18A receipts where eligible.
- Coordinator submits allocation requests tied to specific **PAWS Reference Numbers**.
- Funds are applied to approved costs (e.g., breeder purchase, transport, vet costs) only for dogs verified via the **Anti-Smuggling Protocol**.

### 3. Traceability & Transparency (Robust Clause)
Sponsor acknowledges that funding is strictly tied to the project's traceability standards.
- Every dog supported by these funds will have a verified microchip and a public entry in the PAWS Tracker.
- Sponsor will receive a monthly summary of fund allocation vs. dog reference numbers.

### 4. Sponsor Rights & Restrictions
- Sponsor may be acknowledged as an “Operation PAWS Corporate Sponsor”.
- Sponsor acknowledges that SAPS alone decides dog acceptance; funding does not influence operational selection.
- No operational tactics or sensitive SAPS layouts will be shared with the Sponsor or featured in Sponsor media.

### 5. POPIA Compliance
All parties will protect personal information of donors and breeders in accordance with POPIA.
