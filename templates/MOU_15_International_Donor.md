# MOU-15: International Strategic K9 Donor Agreement
**BETWEEN:** Operation PAWS (National Academy)
**AND:** [INSERT INTERNATIONAL DONOR / KENNEL / POLICE UNIT]

## 1. PURPOSE
This agreement facilitates the transfer of high-drive working dog candidates from international sources to the Operation PAWS National Academy for assessment and BRICS-Standard training.

## 2. LOGISTICS & QUARANTINE
Operation PAWS, in partnership with the Department of Agriculture, Land Reform and Rural Development (DALRRD), ensures "Fast-Track" quarantine protocols for certified PAWS-Strategic imports to maintain dog health and readiness.

## 3. GENETIC PRESERVATION
International donors agree to provide the genetic history of the asset. Operation PAWS reserves the right to utilize the asset in the **National K9 Breeding & Preservation Program** if aptitude scores exceed 95%.

## 4. COSTS
The **International Transport Partner (MOU-09)** shall handle all specialized air-freight logistics, funded by the **League Title Sponsors (MOU-16)**.
