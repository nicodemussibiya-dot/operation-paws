# Reference: SAPS 505(a) (Unconditional Donation)

**Official Title:** Deed of unconditional and irrevocable donation inter vivos.
**Instruction:** SAPS National Instruction 6 of 2018.

## Key Clauses Summary (For PAWS Team)
- **Irrevocable:** Once signed, the donor cannot reclaim the dog.
- **Unconditional:** No strings attached (e.g., "I must be allowed to visit").
- **State Property:** The dog becomes a state asset, registered on the PAS (Asset System).
- **Valuation:** A fair market value is often recorded for audit purposes.

## PAWS Strategy
- We ensure the donor understands this form **before** the final SAPS assessment.
- We do not sign this form on behalf of SAPS; only an authorized SAPS officer (typically at the Unit or Provincial level) may sign as the receiver.
