# MOU-13: K9 Tactical Technology & R&D Partnership
**BETWEEN:** Operation PAWS (Strategic Pipeline)
**AND:** [INSERT R&D PARTNER / e.g. CSIR - Defense & Security]

## 1. PURPOSE
This agreement establishes a framework for the research, development, and deployment of tactical technology designed to protect K9 assets and provide non-lethal overwatch during high-risk operations.

## 2. KEY TECHNOLOGY PILLARS
The Partnership focuses on three critical R&D streams:

### A. Ballistic & Stab Protection (Vests)
*   Development of lightweight, NIJ Level IIIA ballistic vests specifically tailored for the PAWS 3-Tier dog breeds.
*   Integration of GPS tracking and biometric vitals sensors into the vest chassis.

### B. Autonomous K9-Overwatch Drones
*   Development of drones capable of "tetherless tracking" (following the dog's unique infrared signature).
*   High-definition thermal imaging linked directly to the handler's HUD (Heads-Up Display).

### C. Non-Lethal Defense (Active Support)
*   Deployment of "Pepper-Spray Payloads" on drones to neutralize suspects carrying high-caliber weapons (e.g., AK47s), providing the K9 a tactical advantage and reducing the risk of fatal engagement.

## 3. INSURANCE PREMIUM OFFSET
The Insurer (MOU-12) agrees to a **30% reduction in Life Premiums** for any K9 asset deployed with a certified PAWS Ballistic Vest and Active Drone Overwatch.

## 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All tactical data collected during the Academy phase regarding drone/dog synchronization remains the property of the State.
