# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU)
## Operation PAWS – Veterinary Partner

**Parties:**
- **[Foundation / Coordinator legal name]** (“Coordinator”)
- **[Veterinary Practice Name]** (“Veterinary Partner”)

**Effective date:** ____ / ____ / 2026
**Term:** 12 months (renewable)

### 1. Purpose
To provide professional veterinary services, including health assessments, vaccinations, and microchipping for candidate dogs within the Operation PAWS pipeline.

### 2. Traceability & Anti-Smuggling (Robust Clause)
Veterinary Partner acknowledges their critical role in the **Anti-Smuggling & Diversion Protocol**.
- **Microchip Verification:** Veterinary Partner will scan and record the microchip of every dog before any procedure.
- **Reporting:** Any discrepancies in microchip identification must be reported to the Coordinator immediately.
- **Health Certificates:** Official health certificates issued must include the verified microchip number.

### 3. Limitation of Scope
This MOU is strictly limited to the **Operation PAWS** project. It does not create an exclusive partnership or any general agency outside of the veterinary services provided for the project's candidate dogs.

### 4. Responsibilities
**Veterinary Partner will:**
- Conduct thorough physical examinations and temperament observations.
- Administer vaccinations and implant microchips as requested.
- Maintain professional records of all PAWS dogs.

**Coordinator will:**
- Coordinate appointments and provide PAWS Reference Numbers for each dog.
- Ensure prompt payment for services at the agreed project rates.
