### 1. Purpose
To provide the digital infrastructure required to host the Operation PAWS traceability engine, ensuring the project remains welfare-first, secure, and transparent while protecting sensitive operational data.

### 2. Infrastructure Requirements (The "Tech Stack")
The Tech Partner agrees to provide or sponsor the following resources:
- **Private Repository Environment:** Secure hosting for the project's sensitive operational logic, PII-handling scripts, and tactical notes.
- **Supabase / Database Resources:** Hosting for the PAWS 1→500 tracker and anti-smuggling database.
- **Custom Domain & SSL:** Provision of a `.co.za` or `.org.za` domain with high-grade TLS encryption.
- **CDN & High Availability:** Ensuring the QR entry points remain active during high-traffic media campaigns.

### 3. Data Integrity & Security (Crucial)
- **Sensitive Data Ring-Fencing:** Tech Partner acknowledges that certain data (Owner PII, SAPS tactical notes) is strictly confidential and must be hosted in the private, non-public segment of the repository.
- **DDoS Protection:** Protection of the public-facing hub against tampering or malicious downtime.

### 4. Limitation of Scope
This MOU is strictly limited to the **Operation PAWS** project and its Carletonville Pilot. It does not create an exclusive agency or permanent partnership outside of the digital infrastructure services described herein.

### 5. Recognition
Coordinator will acknowledge Tech Partner as the **"Official Infrastructure Sponsor"** on the [Public Tracker](../../docs/tracker/) and the [Start Hub](../../docs/start/).
