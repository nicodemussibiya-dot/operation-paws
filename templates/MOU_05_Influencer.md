# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU)
## Operation PAWS – Influencer / Ambassador Collaboration

**Parties:**
- **[Coordinator/Foundation legal name]** (“Coordinator”)
- **[Influencer full name / entity]** (“Ambassador”)

**Effective date:** ____ / ____ / 2026
**Term:** 90 days (renewable)

### 1. Purpose
To support a public-interest initiative mobilising suitable donation dogs for SAPS K9 capacity using responsible, non-sensational content.

### 2. Ambassador deliverables
- Minimum agreed feed posts, stories, and reels.
- 1 call-to-action linking to the official PAWS entry point (QR Hub).
- Optional attendance at screening/handover days (subject to safety approval).

### 3. Content Rules & Operational Security (Robust Clause)
Ambassador agrees to strict content guardrails:
- No sharing of operational tactics, sensitive layouts, or deployment details.
- **Privacy:** No publishing of personal donor details, addresses, or phone numbers.
- **Traceability:** Content should highlight the importance of microchips and the Anti-Smuggling Protocol.
- **Welfare:** Prioritise messaging that "Not every dog is suitable—screening protects dogs."

### 4. Filming & Consent
No filming of SAPS premises/operations unless written permission is granted by SAPS. Any dog owner featured must sign a filming consent form.

### 5. Lead Handling & POPIA
Ambassador will direct the public to the official intake channel and will not personally collect/store PII from potential donors.

Immediate termination for any breach of operational security, animal welfare standards, or misinformation.
