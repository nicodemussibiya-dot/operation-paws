# MOU-12: Strategic Insurance Partnership
**BETWEEN:** Operation PAWS (National K9 Mobilization Initiative)
**AND:** [INSERT INSURANCE PROVIDER NAME]

## 1. PURPOSE
The purpose of this agreement is to provide comprehensive liability and life coverage for high-value working dog candidates during the "Strategic Pipeline" phase (Screening → Academy → Placement).

## 2. RISK CLASSIFICATION
Insurance premiums and coverage limits are based on the PAWS Strategic Risk Level:

| Role | Risk Level | Coverage Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Patrol & Tracker** | High | Full Life, Occupational Disability, Bite Liability |
| **Explosives Detection** | High | Accidental Death, Frag-Injury Protection |
| **Narcotics Detection** | Medium | Health/Vet, Standard Liability |
| **Search & Rescue** | High | Search Expenses, Accidental Injury |

## 3. DATA SHARING (The "Betting" Factor)
To ensure accurate underwriting, Operation PAWS provides the Insurer with real-time access to the **Academy Progress Logs**. High-aptitude dogs with lower injury history receive reduced premiums.

## 4. CLAIMS & WELFARE
In the event of a "Welfare Veto" by an SPCA official, the policy remains active until the dog is returned to the owner or a welfare sanctuary. 

## 5. RECOGNITION
The Partner shall be listed as the **"Official Guardian of National K9 Assets"** on the PAWS Transparency Dashboard.
