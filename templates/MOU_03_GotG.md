# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU)
## Operation PAWS – Collaboration Framework

**Parties:**
- **[Foundation / Coordinator legal name]** (“Coordinator”)
- **Gift of the Givers Foundation** (“GotG”)

**Effective date:** ____ / ____ / 2026
**Term:** 24 months (renewable)

### 1. Purpose
To collaborate on an ethical, transparent pipeline supporting SAPS K9 capacity by mobilising suitable dogs and funding, starting with the Carletonville pilot.

### 2. Collaboration areas
GotG may support, subject to internal approvals:
- Donation handling frameworks and issuance of Section 18A certificates.
- Credibility oversight and structured reporting of fund allocation.
- Co-branding and communications (subject to mutual approval).

### 3. Anti-Smuggling & Transparency (Robust Clause)
Both parties commit to a "Clean Money, Clean Dog" policy.
- All funding allocation is tied to dogs verified via the **Operation PAWS Anti-Smuggling & Diversion Protocol**.
- Funds are only released for dogs with a verified microchip and a recorded SAPS assessment/acceptance status.
- Audit logs of fund allocation vs dog microchip IDs will be maintained.

### 4. Roles and responsibilities
**Coordinator will:**
- Run lead generation, intake, and partner engagement.
- Maintain project records and the public transparency dashboard.
- Ensure SPCA pre-screen and SAPS assessment steps are respected.

**GotG will (where applicable):**
- Provide an approved donation channel.
- Confirm receipt/tax documentation processes.
- Participate in a joint planning call to align on communications.

### 5. Governance & Welfare
Both parties commit to "Dog Welfare First" and a clear separation between fundraising, procurement, and SAPS acceptance decisions.
