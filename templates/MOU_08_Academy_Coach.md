### 1. Purpose
To provide professional training and conditioning for Tier 3 (Academy) candidate dogs to prepare them for official SAPS K9 assessments.

### 2. Training Standards & Welfare
- Coach agrees to follow the **Operation PAWS Academy Curriculum**.
- Welfare is paramount: only humane, reward-based, and evidence-based training methods are permitted.
- Any signs of injury or health issues must be reported to the Coordinator immediately.

### 3. Traceability & Anti-Smuggling (Robust Clause)
Coach is responsible for the custody of dogs during training sessions.
- **Microchip Check:** Coach must scan and verify the dog’s microchip at the start and end of every training period/session.
- **Chain of Custody:** Coach must sign the custody log when receiving or handing back a dog.
- **Reporting:** Any unauthorized movement of a dog is a material breach of this agreement.

### 4. Limitation of Scope
This MOU is strictly limited to the **Operation PAWS** project. Coach is an independent contractor for this project and this agreement does not create an employment relationship or a broader partnership.

### 5. Media & Confidentiality
Coach will not share training videos or photos featuring PAWS dogs without written approval and must never show sensitive tactical training or layouts.
