# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU)
## Operation PAWS – SPCA Screening Partner

**Parties:**
- **[Foundation / Coordinator legal name]** (“Coordinator”)
- **[Local SPCA full legal name]** (“SPCA”)

**Effective date:** ____ / ____ / 2026
**Term:** 12 months (renewable by written agreement)

### 1. Purpose
To support the SAPS K9 Donation Drive by providing basic welfare screening and an initial temperament/suitability pre-screen for candidate dogs before SAPS conducts operational assessment.

### 2. Scope of SPCA pre-screen
SPCA may assess: general welfare, visible health concerns, microchip scan, basic handling tolerance, basic temperament indicators, and any immediate red flags. SPCA will issue a brief outcome note: “Pre-screen passed / not recommended”.

### 3. Anti-Smuggling & Traceability (Robust Clause)
Both parties agree to adhere to the **Operation PAWS Anti-Smuggling & Diversion Protocol**. 
- SPCA must scan the dog’s microchip at the start of the pre-screen and record the number.
- Any dog without a microchip must be chipped by SPCA (at agreed cost) before proceeding.
- No dog shall be transferred without a recorded microchip scan verification.

### 4. Roles and responsibilities
**Coordinator will:**
- Schedule screenings; obtain owner consent; manage logistics.
- Maintain a single intake record for each dog and share only necessary details with SPCA/SAPS.
- Ensure all dogs are microchipped prior to or at the time of screening.

**SPCA will:**
- Perform pre-screen ethically and safely.
- Flag urgent welfare concerns and advise owners appropriately.
- Maintain a custody log for any animal temporarily held on SPCA premises.

### 5. Outcome handling
If a dog does not pass SPCA pre-screen, the dog remains the owner/breeder’s responsibility. SPCA may, at its discretion, provide welfare guidance and/or rehoming advice.

### 6. Media / filming
No filming at SPCA premises or of SPCA staff occurs without prior written approval from SPCA and written consent from the dog’s owner.

### 7. Confidentiality & POPIA
Both parties will protect personal information and use it only for the purpose of this project, aligned with POPIA.

### 8. Limitation of Scope
This MOU is strictly limited to the **Operation PAWS** project. It does not create any general partnership, agency, or employment relationship between the parties outside of the specific deliverables of this project.

### 9. Termination
Either party may terminate with 30 days’ written notice. Immediate termination is permitted for serious breach of welfare or anti-smuggling protocols.
