### 1. Purpose
To provide safe, secure, and humane transport for candidate dogs between owners, SPCA, Academy sites, and SAPS assessment centers.

### 2. Transport Safety & Welfare
- Dogs must be transported in well-ventilated, secure, and clean crates.
- Transport Partner must ensure dogs have access to water and appropriate rest breaks for long-distance moves.
- Vehicles must be climate-controlled or appropriately ventilated for South African conditions.

### 3. Traceability & Anti-Smuggling (Robust Clause)
Transport Partner is a vital link in the **Chain of Custody**.
- **Microchip Scan at Pickup:** Transport Partner must scan the dog and verify it matches the PAWS Waybill before loading.
- **Microchip Scan at Drop-off:** Both Transport Partner and Receiver must scan the dog to verify correct delivery.
- **Waybill System:** A digital or physical waybill must be signed at both ends of every trip.

### 4. Limitation of Scope
This MOU is strictly limited to the **Operation PAWS** project. It does not create an exclusive transport agreement or a general agency.

### 5. Confidentiality
Transport Partner will not disclose pickup/drop-off locations or timing to unauthorized third parties to protect operational security.
